Zero Data Collection
🔒

Zero Data Collection. Zero Tracking. Zero Compromise.

Sukoon does not collect, store, sell, or share any personal data. We don't want your data. We want you to find peace.

Our Promise

Sukoon was built on a simple belief: mental health tools should be free, private, and accessible to every human on Earth. We don't monetize your attention, your emotions, or your personal information. We exist solely to bring peace to those who need it.

What We DON'T Do

  • No accounts or sign-ups. You never need to register. Ever.
  • No email collection. We don't ask for your email address.
  • No cookies (except a single cookie to remember your language preference).
  • No analytics or tracking. No Google Analytics, no Mixpanel, no Meta Pixel, no fingerprinting.
  • No advertising. No ads. No sponsored content. No affiliate links.
  • No data selling. We have nothing to sell because we collect nothing.

Your Journal & Mood Data

When you use the Mood Tracker and Journal, your entries are stored using IndexedDB -- a storage system built into your web browser. This means:

  • Your journal entries never leave your device.
  • They are not sent to any server -- not ours, not anyone else's.
  • Only you can see them. Only you can delete them.
  • You can export your data as JSON anytime -- it's your data, your choice.
  • If you clear your browser data, your journal entries will be erased.

AI Friend Conversations

When you talk to the AI Friend:

  • Your conversation is processed in real-time and not stored on our server.
  • Messages are sent to an AI model (Anthropic Claude) to generate responses.
  • The conversation exists only in your browser session.
  • When you close the tab or refresh the page, the conversation is gone forever.
  • We do not log, record, or analyze any conversations.

GDPR Compliance

Sukoon is designed to be GDPR-compliant by default because we follow the simplest privacy principle: don't collect data in the first place.

Open Source Transparency

Sukoon's code is open and transparent. You can inspect exactly what data flows where. There are no hidden trackers, no backdoors, no surprises. Every line of code is written with your privacy in mind.

Children's Privacy

Sukoon does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. Since we don't collect data from anyone, this is inherently true. However, if you are a minor, we encourage you to use Sukoon with a parent or guardian's knowledge.

Contact

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, reach out to us. We're humans who care about your wellbeing -- not a corporation mining your data for profit.
